Introduction

This Agreement is entered into between Unity JAX Private Training ("Trainer," "We") and the Client identified in the signature block below ("Client," "You"). By signing, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to all terms contained herein. This Agreement governs all virtual personal training services provided by Unity JAX Private Training.

Section 1 — Assumption of Risk & Liability Waiver

1.1 Voluntary Participation. Client acknowledges that participation in personal training and physical fitness activities involves inherent risks including physical injury, muscle strain, joint damage, cardiovascular events, and in extreme cases, death. Client voluntarily assumes all such risks.

1.2 Health Clearance. Client represents they are in adequate physical health to participate in an exercise program and has disclosed all known medical conditions, injuries, and limitations on their intake form. Unity JAX Private Training strongly recommends physician clearance before beginning any program.

1.3 Release of Liability. To the fullest extent permitted by Florida law, Client releases, waives, and discharges Unity JAX Private Training, its trainers, representatives, and affiliates from any and all liability, claims, or causes of action arising out of or related to any loss, damage, injury, or death sustained in connection with training services.

1.4 Virtual Training Acknowledgment. Client understands all training is delivered virtually. Unity JAX Private Training is not physically present during workouts and cannot directly supervise exercise execution. Client accepts full responsibility for performing exercises safely and stopping immediately if pain or discomfort occurs.

Section 2 — Minimum 3-Month Commitment

Client agrees to a minimum commitment period of three (3) consecutive months from the date of program enrollment. This applies to all plan tiers: Foundation, Transformation, and Elite.

2.1 Rationale. The 3-month minimum exists because meaningful physical transformation requires consistent effort over time. Unity JAX Private Training invests significant time into each Client's custom program design, ongoing assessments, and weekly check-ins.

2.2 Early Termination. If Client wishes to terminate prior to completing the 3-month minimum, Client remains financially responsible for the full value of the remaining commitment period.

2.3 Renewal. After the initial commitment period, services continue month-to-month unless Client provides written notice of cancellation at least 14 days prior to the next billing date.

Section 3 — Payment & No Refund Policy

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Unity JAX Private Training operates a strict no-refund policy. Once payment has been received and a training program has been created and delivered, no refunds will be issued under any circumstances.

This includes but is not limited to:

  • Change of mind or personal circumstances
  • Inability to complete workouts due to schedule conflicts
  • Dissatisfaction with results (results depend on Client effort and adherence)
  • Medical issues arising after enrollment
  • Failure to log in or use the program

Dispute Resolution. Client agrees to contact Unity JAX Private Training directly at info@unitytrainingjax.com before initiating any chargeback. Unauthorized chargebacks will be contested.

Section 4 — Scope of Services

Unity JAX Private Training agrees to provide: (a) a customized virtual training program based on Client's intake information; (b) weekly check-in communications; (c) daily trainer availability via designated messaging for questions and support; (d) program updates based on Client progress. Unity JAX Private Training does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Section 5 — Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Duval County, Florida.
